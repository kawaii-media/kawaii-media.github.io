GooLex Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 4/1/2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use GooLex. We are committed to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Google Play Store policies.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data (User-Provided Information)

  • Name, email address, and payment details (only if you subscribe to premium features).
  • Any communications you send to us (e.g., support requests).

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (e.g., pages visited, time spent, clicks).
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and browser type.
  • Log Data: IP address, crash reports, app performance statistics.

1.3 Third-Party Data Collection

We use third-party services to improve our application experience:

  • Google Analytics & Firebase Crash Analytics: To analyze usage patterns and improve performance.
  • AdMob: To serve ads to non-subscribed users.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain GooLex's functionality.
  • Improve user experience through analytics.
  • Deliver personalized ads to non-subscribers.
  • Process subscription payments and manage accounts.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

  • Consent: When you agree to data collection for analytics and advertising.
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve and secure our services.
  • Contractual Obligation: To provide premium features to subscribed users.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with laws and regulations.

4. Advertising & Analytics

4.1 Google AdMob (For Non-Subscribed Users)

We display ads using Google AdMob, which may collect data such as device identifiers and browsing behavior. You can opt out of personalized ads via Google's Ad Settings.

4.2 Google Analytics & Firebase Crash Analytics

We use Google Analytics and Firebase Crash Analytics to monitor app performance and troubleshoot errors.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. However, we share data with:

  • Service Providers: For analytics, payment processing, and app functionality.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights.

All third parties comply with GDPR and Play Store policies.

6. Data Retention & Security

  • We retain your data only as long as necessary for our services.
  • Personal data linked to subscriptions is stored as long as your account is active.
  • We use encryption, firewalls, and security protocols to protect your data.

7. Children's Privacy

GooLex is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of GooLex means acceptance of the updated policy.
